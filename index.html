Praharsh Nanavati

I am an incoming Doctoral Student at the RTG on Neuroexplicit Models at Saarland University. I was previously a Predoctoral Researcher at the CSA Department at IISc, working on Causal Fairness in Machine Learning. Before that, I was an integrated BS-MS Student at IISERB.

I am fortunate to have been advised by Dr. Siddharth Barman at IIsc, and co-advised by Dr. Karthikeyan Shanmugam (Google Deepmind, India) and Dr. Ranjitha Prasad (IIITD) as an external visiting student for my Master's thesis. I have worked in causal representation learning (CRL), causal discovery, explainable artificial intelligence (XAI), probabilistic models, and sports analytics.
